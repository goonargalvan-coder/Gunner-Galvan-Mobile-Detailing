Exterior

Exterior Detail

A thorough exterior service that removes dirt, grime, brake dust, and road film — then protects your paint and restores the shine. Every surface is cleaned and treated, from the roof down to the rocker panels.

1

Pre-rinse & foam soak — heavy soil loosened with a full foam cannon pre-soak before any contact washing begins.

2

Hand wash (two-bucket method) — safe, swirl-minimizing wash technique using premium car soap on all painted surfaces.

3

Wheel & tire deep clean — wheel faces, spokes, and barrels scrubbed; brake dust removed; tires cleaned to the sidewall.

4

Wheel well rinse — fender wells sprayed down and decontaminated to remove packed road grime and salt residue.

5

Exterior glass cleaning — all windows and mirrors cleaned streak-free inside the exterior pass.

6

Tire dressing — tires finished with a clean, satin-look dressing for a uniform, professional appearance.

7

Spray wax & detail spray — paint sealed with a protective spray wax layer, then finished with a quick detail spray for maximum gloss.

Interior

Interior Detail

A deep-clean interior service that goes far beyond a standard vacuum. Every surface, crevice, and fabric panel gets individual attention — leaving your cabin looking and smelling as close to new as possible.

1

Full vacuum — seats, floor mats, carpets, trunk, under seats, and all crevices vacuumed thoroughly.

2

Floor mat deep clean — rubber and carpet mats removed, scrubbed, and dried before reinstalling.

3

Seat & surface wipe-down — fabric seats, door panels, and hard surfaces wiped down to remove dirt, grime, and buildup.

4

Dashboard & console wipe-down — every surface including vents, cup holders, knobs, and buttons cleaned and conditioned.

5

Door panels & jambs — door cards, handles, and door jambs cleaned and dressed to match the rest of the interior.

6

Leather conditioning (if applicable) — leather surfaces cleaned and conditioned to prevent cracking and restore suppleness.

7

Interior glass cleaning — all interior windows and the windshield cleaned streak-free.
